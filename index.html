Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between C3 and H3?

C3 focuses on personalized spiritual coaching and leadership development, while H3 is centered on healing and restoration through guided prayer and inner work. Both are Christ-centered but serve different stages of your spiritual journey.

How do I know which service is right for me?

During your initial consultation, we help assess where you are and what you need—whether that’s mentorship (C3), healing (H3), or learning resources from the E-Library. Every step is prayerfully guided.

Is Candlelight Services affiliated with a specific church?

While we operate with strong Christian values and principles, Candlelight Services is an independent ministry and not tied to any single denomination. Our focus is Christ, not church labels.

Are your services available online?

Yes! Most of our sessions—whether C3 mentorship or H3 healing programs—are available virtually. Our E-Library is also fully digital and accessible 24/7 worldwide.

Is there a cost for the services?

Many of our foundational resources are free or donation-based. Some coaching or in-depth sessions may carry a fee, but we’re committed to making support accessible regardless of finances.

Can’t find an answer to your question?

If you're still unsure about how to get started, our team is here to help. Whether it's prayer, coaching, or content support—we're just a message away.
